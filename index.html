Simple Calories - Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2, 2025

Overview

Simple Calories is a calorie tracking app that respects your privacy. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Storage

All data you enter in the app (food entries, calorie goals, and settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your phone.

Data Collection

We do not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

Third-Party Services

Simple Calories does not use any third-party services that collect data.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.
